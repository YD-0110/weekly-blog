Places Must Visit

rashays

i had visited in august 2021

Rashays is a popular restaraunt in various places of sydney. Their menu has mouth melting dishes that satifies your hunger. Many crowds has preffered it over other fast food shops. The food is amazing, with large dishes at a low price. Rashays also provides many drinks and free food for kids at different times. They also give a free sweet drink to all kids ordering from the kids menu.
